Meet the founder

Built by someone who's
been in your boots.

Before TDN Digital, I spent years as a self-employed swimming pool engineer — working on commercial and industrial projects, running my own jobs, managing my own clients. I know exactly what it's like to be a one-man-band trying to juggle the work and the business at the same time.

What I kept seeing was small businesses — builders, cleaners, tradespeople — with brilliant skills and terrible websites. They were losing enquiries to competitors who just happened to have a better online presence. Not because they were better. Just because they were easier to find and easier to contact.

I started TDN Digital to fix that. To give local businesses access to the kind of modern websites and AI tools that big companies take for granted — at a price that actually makes sense. I work directly with every client. No sales team, no outsourcing, no account managers. Just me, doing the work.
